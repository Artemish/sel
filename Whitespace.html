E.2 Whitespace

  • no tabs
  • no closing parenthesis on lines by themselves
  • indent everything as would GNU Emacs
  • typically only include vertical whitespace between top-level forms, sections of large functions may be demarcated by vertical whitespace but it is better to use smaller functions
  • no trailing whitespace
  • no whitespace following an open-paren
